Light Bulb Placement Game

Time Elapsed :

Once upon a time, King Unnamed of Nowhereland was both smiling and crying at the same time. He was smiling because his enormous brand-new palace with many spacious rooms and corridors has just been finished. He is also crying as these rooms need to be illuminated and kept warm, but the ongoing increase of utility costs affect him as well. So now it's time to think about the placement of the palace's light bulbs. We need to place them so that everything is properly lit, but we cannot install any unnecessary bulbs. You can start the game by choosing one of the maps (according to their difficulty) You can save, load or refresh your game by pressing the according buttons. Once one map is generated, this text will disappear and your map will come to the screen. The game has autosaving feature, whenever you place a lightbulb the game saves it for you, so you don't need to worry about losing your progress. You can also see your old progress by pressing the load button. Good Luck!
